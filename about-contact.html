Our Story

Welcome to Chitralaya ‚Äî a digital art gallery that brings the beauty of original artworks directly to your home. The name "Chitralaya" means "House of Pictures" in Sanskrit, and that's exactly what we are ‚Äî a curated collection of beautiful, handcrafted artworks.

Every piece in our gallery is an original creation, lovingly crafted by my mother. Each artwork tells a story, captures an emotion, and brings a unique piece of artistry into your space. From vibrant canvas paintings depicting divine deities and serene landscapes to intricate charcoal sketches and delicate watercolor paintings, our collection represents years of dedication and passion for art.

Our Mission

At Chitralaya, our mission is simple yet profound:

  • To make authentic, original art accessible to art lovers everywhere
  • To celebrate the beauty of traditional and contemporary Indian art
  • To connect art enthusiasts with unique, handcrafted pieces
  • To preserve and share the joy of artistic expression

What We Offer

Canvas Paintings

Our canvas collection features stunning paintings of Hindu deities, landscapes, and traditional motifs. Each piece is created with attention to detail and vibrant colors that bring the artwork to life.

Charcoal Sketches

Intricate and detailed charcoal sketches showcase portraits, wildlife, and everyday scenes. These monochrome masterpieces demonstrate the power of contrast and shading.

Watercolor Paintings

Delicate and ethereal watercolor paintings capture the beauty of nature, architecture, and abstract concepts with soft, flowing colors.
