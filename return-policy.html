Return Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Return Eligibility

At Chitralaya, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. We accept returns for artworks within 7 days of delivery, provided the following conditions are met:

  • The artwork must be in its original condition, unused, and unaltered
  • The artwork must be in its original packaging with all tags and certificates intact
  • The artwork must not show any signs of damage, wear, or handling
  • Custom or personalized artworks are not eligible for return unless there is a manufacturing defect

2. Return Process

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact Us: Email us at mannu131376@gmail.com within 7 days of receiving your order with your order number and reason for return.
  2. Return Authorization: We will review your request and provide you with a Return Authorization (RA) number within 2-3 business days.
  3. Packaging: Securely package the artwork in its original packaging. If original packaging is not available, use appropriate protective materials to prevent damage during transit.
  4. Shipping: Ship the artwork to the address provided in your return authorization email. The customer is responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to our error or a defective product.
  5. Inspection: Once we receive the returned artwork, we will inspect it within 3-5 business days.

3. Refund Processing

Upon approval of your return:

  • Refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 7-10 business days after we receive and inspect the returned artwork
  • Original shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error
  • Return shipping costs will be deducted from the refund amount unless the return is due to a defect or our error
  • You will receive an email confirmation once the refund has been processed

4. Non-Returnable Items

The following items are not eligible for return:

  • Custom or personalized artworks (unless defective)
  • Artworks that have been damaged due to misuse or mishandling by the customer
  • Items returned after the 7-day return period
  • Items without original packaging or tags
  • Digital products or downloadable content

5. Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective artwork:

  • Contact us immediately at mannu131376@gmail.com within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide photos of the damage or defect
  • We will arrange for a replacement or full refund, including return shipping costs
  • We may request the return of the damaged item for quality control purposes

6. Exchange Policy

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. If you wish to exchange an item:

  • Return the original item following our return process
  • Place a new order for the desired artwork
  • We will process your refund for the returned item separately

7. Contact Information

For any questions regarding returns, please contact us:

  • Email: mannu131376@gmail.com
  • Response Time: We aim to respond to all return inquiries within 2-3 business days

8. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this return policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
