Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Refund Eligibility

Chitralaya offers refunds for eligible purchases under the following circumstances:

  • Returns initiated within 7 days of delivery and meeting our return policy requirements
  • Damaged or defective items received
  • Items that differ significantly from the description on our website
  • Orders cancelled before shipment
  • Duplicate charges or billing errors

2. Refund Processing Time

Once we receive and inspect your returned item:

  • Inspection Period: 3-5 business days after receiving the returned artwork
  • Refund Processing: 7-10 business days after approval
  • Total Timeline: Refunds typically appear in your account within 10-15 business days from the date we receive your return
  • You will receive an email confirmation once the refund has been processed

3. Refund Method

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Refunds will be credited to the original card within 7-10 business days
  • UPI Payments: Refunds will be processed to the original UPI account
  • Net Banking: Refunds will be credited to the original bank account
  • Digital Wallets: Refunds will be processed to the original wallet account
  • Please note that it may take additional time for your bank or payment provider to process and post the refund to your account

4. Refund Amount

The refund amount will include:

  • Full purchase price of the returned item
  • Original shipping charges (only if the return is due to our error or a defective product)
  • Return shipping costs (only if the return is due to our error or a defective product)

The refund amount will NOT include:

  • Original shipping charges (for standard returns)
  • Return shipping costs (for standard returns)
  • Any handling or processing fees

5. Cancelled Orders

If you cancel an order before it has been shipped:

  • You will receive a full refund, including shipping charges
  • Refunds for cancelled orders are processed within 3-5 business days
  • You will receive an email confirmation of the cancellation and refund

If an order is cancelled after shipment, it will be treated as a return and subject to our return policy terms.

6. Non-Refundable Items

The following items or situations are not eligible for refunds:

  • Custom or personalized artworks (unless defective)
  • Items returned after the 7-day return period
  • Items damaged due to customer misuse or mishandling
  • Items returned without original packaging or in unsellable condition
  • Digital products or downloadable content
  • Gift cards or promotional vouchers (unless required by law)

7. Partial Refunds

In certain circumstances, we may issue partial refunds:

  • If the returned item shows signs of use or damage not present at the time of shipment
  • If the item is missing parts or accessories
  • If the item is returned in a condition that reduces its value
  • You will be notified via email if a partial refund is issued, along with the reason

8. Refund Disputes

If you have concerns about your refund:

  • Contact us immediately at mannu131376@gmail.com
  • Provide your order number and details of the issue
  • We will investigate and respond within 3-5 business days
  • We are committed to resolving all refund disputes fairly and promptly

9. Currency and Exchange Rates

All refunds will be processed in the same currency as the original transaction. If currency conversion occurred during the original payment, the refund amount may differ slightly due to exchange rate fluctuations. We are not responsible for any differences in exchange rates.

10. Contact Information

For refund inquiries or assistance, please contact us:

  • Email: mannu131376@gmail.com
  • Subject Line: "Refund Inquiry - Order #[Your Order Number]"
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 2-3 business days

11. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this refund policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the modified policy.
